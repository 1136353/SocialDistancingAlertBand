Social Distancing Alert Band (SDAB) 

the idea is all about making people habitual to follow social distancing. 

Problem :
Many people in our country not following social distancing. 
And those who are following social distancing they have a bad habit of touching their face again and again.

Solution :
Social Distancing Alert Band (SDAB) 
Social distancing alert band is giving the user continuous alert and follow social distancing and not to touch your face.
Two-person keep the distance between them is 1m, if less than 1m buzzer will ON.
